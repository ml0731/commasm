BLUELOCK

Based on the popular soccer anime Blue Lock ブルーロック

Blue Lock is a training facility that was built for the purpose of inviting prodigious Japanese football players,
who would live in the facility and follow the strict personally-designed training regime created by Jinpachi Ego.

After a disastrous defeat at the 2018 World Cup, Japan's team struggles to regroup. But what's missing? An absolute Ace Striker, who can guide them to the win. The Japan Football Union is hell-bent on creating a striker who hungers for goals and thirsts for victory, and who can be the decisive instrument in turning around a losing match... and to do so, they've gathered 300 of Japan's best and brightest youth players. Who will emerge to lead the team... and will they be able to out-muscle and out-ego everyone who stands in their way?
